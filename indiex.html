Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Can't find your answer? Call us at 8527533067

Shri Radha Sky Gardens offers 2 BHK (Type B – 1182 Sq.ft, Type C – 1286 Sq.ft) and 3 BHK (Type A & B – 1465 Sq.ft each) apartments. 4 BHK options are also available — please contact our sales team for details.
Two attractive plans are available. The Special Payment Plan follows a 10:10:75:5 ratio — 10% at booking, 10% within 30 days, 75% at possession, and 5% at registry. The Subvention Plan follows 10:80:10 — you pay 10% upfront, the bank funds 80% (EMI-free till possession), and 10% at possession.
The project is currently under construction with an attractive possession timeline. Under the Subvention Plan, you will be EMI-free until possession, making it a great time to invest now. Contact our team at 8527533067 for the latest possession status and construction updates.
The project features 10+ premium amenities: Modern Swimming Pool, Fully Equipped Gymnasium, Kids Play Area, Jogging Track, Card Play Room, High-Speed Elevators, Covered Car Parking, 24×7 CCTV Security, Sports Facilities, and a Beautiful Fountain Area — all within a secure gated community.
Booking a site visit is simple! Fill out the enquiry form above, or call us directly at 8527533067. Our relationship manager will schedule a convenient time, arrange complimentary transportation if needed, and ensure you get a complete walkthrough of the project and sample flat.
Additional charges include OTLR (One-Time Lease Rent) at ₹50/Sq.ft and IFMS (Interest-Free Maintenance Security) at ₹20/Sq.ft. Stamp duty, registration, and other statutory charges will also apply as per government norms. For a complete cost breakdown, request a callback from our team.
